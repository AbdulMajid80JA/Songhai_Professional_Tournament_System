# 🎉 SONGHAI SALLAH FOOTBALL GALA TOURNAMENT SYSTEM

## 📌 Overview
The **Songhai Sallah Football Gala Tournament System** is a web-based platform developed to support the organization and management of the **SONGHAI SALLAH FOOTBALL GALA TOURNAMENT**, a community football event organized by **Saturday Fada Ziorme** in Aflao, Ghana.

The tournament started in **2022** and is organized **twice every year** during **Eid El Fitr** and **Eid Al Adha**, with the aim of promoting **Muslim unity, brotherhood, and inclusivity**, while standing firmly against tribal discrimination of any kind.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the System
This system is designed to:
- Enable easy **team registration**
- Display a **gallery of previous tournament editions**
- Provide administrators with a **dashboard to manage registered teams**
- Support transparency, organization, and growth of the tournament

The tournament has **zero tolerance for discrimination or misconduct**, and appropriate measures are taken if any action goes against its values.

---

## 🖥️ Main Pages & Features

### 📝 Register
- Allows teams to register for the tournament
- Collects and stores essential team information
- Registration data is available for admin review

---

### 🖼️ Gallery
- Showcases photos and media from previous editions
- Preserves the history and spirit of the tournament
- Highlights moments from Eid celebrations

---

### 👥 Teams (Admin Dashboard)
- Displays all registered teams
- Accessible to administrators only
- Supports management and oversight of team data

---

## 👤 User Roles

### Public Users
- View tournament information
- Register teams
- Browse the gallery

### Admin
- Access the teams dashboard
- View and manage registered teams
- Monitor registration activities

---

## 🤝 Core Values
- Brotherhood and unity among Muslims
- Inclusivity across all tribes and backgrounds
- Respect, discipline, and fair play
