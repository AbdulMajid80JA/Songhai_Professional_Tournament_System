Tournament Rules & Regulations

Official handbook governing participation, discipline, competition format and prize structure for the Songhai Sallah Football Gala Tournament.

General Competition Rules
⚽ Maximum 25 players per team
📝 Registered players only may participate
🧑‍⚖️ Referees’ decisions are final
Player Eligibility Rules
✅ All players must be registered before tournament kickoff
🪪 Players may be required to present identification
🚫 A player cannot represent more than one team
❌ Fielding unregistered players may lead to forfeiture
Protest Procedure
📢 Protests must be lodged immediately after the match
📄 All protests must be submitted to organizers officially
⚖️ Tournament committee rulings are final
Discipline Sanctions
🟨 Accumulated misconduct may trigger suspension
🟥 Red card offences may lead to automatic ban
⛔ Violence or serious misconduct may cause disqualification
Knockout Tie-Break Rules
🥅 Group standings determined by points, goal difference and goals scored
⏱️ Knockout drawn matches proceed to penalties
🏆 Penalty shootouts determine advancement where required
Prize Structure
🥇 Champions — Trophy + Gold Prize
🥈 Runner-Up — Silver Prize
🥉 Third Place — Bronze Prize
⭐ Best Player Award
🧤 Best Goalkeeper Award
⚽ Top Scorer Award
Important Notice

The organizing committee reserves the right to interpret, amend and enforce these regulations in the interest of fair play and tournament integrity.
