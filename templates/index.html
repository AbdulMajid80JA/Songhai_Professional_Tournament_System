About Us

The Songhai Sallah Football Gala Tournament is a community-driven initiative designed to promote unity, youth empowerment, and sporting excellence. Founded by passionate football stakeholders, this tournament brings together teams from different regions to compete, connect, and celebrate culture through sport.

Our mission is to create opportunities for young talents, strengthen community bonds, and provide a platform where football becomes a tool for growth, discipline, and success.
